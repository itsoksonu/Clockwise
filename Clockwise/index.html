Our Services

At Clockwise, we specialize in providing comprehensive career counseling and development services for school students. We understand that choosing a career path can be a daunting task, especially for young individuals who are just starting to explore their interests and aptitudes. Our goal is to empower students by equipping them with the necessary guidance and tools to make informed decisions about their future.

How it works

Clockwise is lot more than assessment and counselling.

Your Career Journey in Simple Steps ↓

01

Consultation

The process begins with an initial consultation, where we gather information about the student's background, interests, and aspirations. This consultation helps us understand their unique needs and allows us to tailor our services accordingly.

02

Assessments

Based on the consultation, we administer a series of assessments designed to assess the student's interests, strengths, and values. These assessments provide valuable insights into their career preferences and form the foundation for personalized guidance.

03

Counseling Sessions

Our experienced career counselors conduct one-on-one sessions with students, where they delve deeper into the assessment results and explore various career options. These sessions provide an opportunity for students to ask questions, discuss concerns, and gain a comprehensive understanding of the available pathways.

04

Action Plan

After identifying suitable career options, we work collaboratively with the student to create a customized action plan. This plan outlines the steps required to achieve their career goals, including academic milestones, skill development, and extracurricular activities.

05

Ongoing Support

Our commitment doesn't end with the creation of an action plan. We provide ongoing support and guidance to ensure students stay on track. We offer follow-up sessions, progress tracking, and additional resources to address any challenges or changes that may arise along their career journey.
